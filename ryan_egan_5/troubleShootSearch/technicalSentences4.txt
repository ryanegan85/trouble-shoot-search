If a red light is flashing on the drive, the drive has reached capacity.
If the connecting cable is not fitting in either port, the wire may be defective and need to be replaced.
If data on the drive becomes corrupted, hold the button on the back for 5 seconds to wipe all data.
In the case of a broken drive, you can get a replacement if it is under warranty.
If a game is slow or not running properly while installed on the drive, try to repair the installation in the options menu.
If an installed game cannot be found, try unistalling and reinstalling it in the options menu.
