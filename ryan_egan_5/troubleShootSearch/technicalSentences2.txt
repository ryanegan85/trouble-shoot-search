If your cursor is freezing, your firmware may be out of date.
If you are getting a blue screen of death, try updating the firmware.
If your computer won't boot, make sure the SSD is mounted correctly.
If files cannot be written, your drive may have reached its write capacity.
If an app is crashing frequently, it may not be compatible with your drive.
If you are frequently prompted to repair the drive, you may have a bad block that must be deleted.
